# Privacy Policy

_Last updated: 2026-04-19_

Welcome to **hyperg8**. This privacy policy explains what information we collect when you visit
our website and play our games, and what we do with it. We aim to collect as little as possible.

## 1. Who we are

hyperg8 is an independent game studio. You can reach us at **hello@hyperg8.com** for any privacy
related question.

## 2. What we collect on this website

This site is a static one-pager. We do **not** set marketing cookies, we do **not** profile you,
and we do **not** sell any data. Your browser may send standard request headers (IP, User-Agent)
to our hosting provider for the purpose of serving the page.

## 3. External links

The "Play it on" buttons take you to third-party storefronts (Steam, CrazyGames, Google Play,
App Store, wavedash, itch.io). Once you follow a link, the privacy policy of that platform
applies. We have no control over what they collect.

## 4. Our games

Each individual game lists its own data handling inside the game itself. In general we only
process what is required to make the game work (save files, anonymous crash reports, anonymous
gameplay statistics).

## 5. Your rights

If we hold any personal data about you, you may at any time request access, correction or
deletion by writing to **hello@hyperg8.com**. We will respond within a reasonable timeframe.

We may update this policy. The date at the top of the document reflects the latest revision.
